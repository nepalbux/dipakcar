About Dipak Car Decoration

Crafting automotive excellence since our inception

Our Story

Dipak Car Decoration has been at the forefront of automotive styling and customization in Pokhara, Nepal. Founded with a passion for transforming ordinary vehicles into extraordinary masterpieces, we have built our reputation on quality craftsmanship, innovative designs, and exceptional customer service.

Located in the heart of Nayabazar, Pokhara-8, our state-of-the-art facility serves as the premier destination for car enthusiasts seeking professional decoration and modification services. We specialize in all kinds of vehicle decoration, from subtle enhancements to complete transformations.
