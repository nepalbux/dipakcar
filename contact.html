Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What services do you offer?

We offer complete car decoration services including interior styling, exterior modifications, alloy wheel installation, door visors, chrome accessories, wedding car decoration, and audio system installations.

How long does installation take?

Installation time varies by service. Simple accessories like door visors take 1-2 hours, while complete decoration packages may take 1-2 days. We'll provide accurate timing during consultation.

Do you provide warranty?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranty on all our work and products. Warranty periods vary by service type and will be clearly explained before work begins.

Can I get a quote before visiting?

Absolutely! Contact us via phone or WhatsApp with your vehicle details and requirements. We'll provide an initial quote and schedule a consultation for detailed assessment.
